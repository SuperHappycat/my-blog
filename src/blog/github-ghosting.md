---
title: Ghosting on GitHub
date: 2026-05-26
---

Have we ever considered the point that sometimes we ghost people on GitHub too? We see a pull request and then look at its atrocity and decide not to respond. At least answer it! These people have poured at least *some* time on the PR, whether or not it was just prompting AI. We should probably try to advocate for less ghosting on GitHub.
